manage your download connections with IVANS Exchange
Join the webinar and learn about IVANS Exchange, a new online tool to view and manage your carrier download. IVANS Exchange offers acomprehensive view of your current and potential download activity with carriers to increase the efficiency and profitability of your agency.

Discover the benefits of IVANS Exchange:

  • Agency Connectivity Dashboard: The graphical, customized dashboard provides you with a single view into your current and available carrier download connections, download transaction volume, and newly activated lines of business downloads by carrier.
  • IVANS Connections Report: A personalized report allows you to review download connections that are inactive or have yet to be activated. Updated daily with the latest carrier download offerings, you can customize the report to:
    • Indicate available lines of business interest for future download
    • Mark lines of business that are not currently available from select carriers but would be interest once available
    • Note lines of business that you are actively choosing not to download and why
  • Agency Download Usage Reports: Usage reports identify the number of download transactions you are receiving per line of business and per carrier.
